# Mode: outline

## Steps

1. Confirm research question, scope, and available final figures/tables.
2. Apply evidence and conflict gates from `SKILL.md`.
3. Record the central contribution contract (see below).
4. If Results are in scope, build the reverse figure outline
   ([reference.md](../reference.md#reverse-figure-outline)).
5. Build the paragraph storyboard for each planned section
   ([reference.md](../reference.md#paragraph-storyboard)).
6. Use an existing `manuscript/outline.md` when present; otherwise
   [assets/manuscript-outline.md](../assets/manuscript-outline.md).
   Do not create a second source of truth.

**Done when** central contribution, result logic (if Results in scope), and
paragraph storyboard are written, and unresolved evidence or decisions are
listed explicitly.

Skip the storyboard for surgical edits, a single isolated paragraph,
captions, or proofreading — those are not this mode.

## Central contribution contract

| Field | Question |
|---|---|
| Research question | What exact question does the study answer? |
| Answer | What do the results establish? |
| Contribution | What changes because this study exists? |
| Evidence | Which result IDs, figures, or tables support the answer? |
| Boundary | What is the strongest claim the design and evidence permit? |
| Significance | Why does this contribution matter beyond the study? |

Every main figure, Results subsection, and Discussion claim should establish,
qualify, or interpret this contribution.

## Output

1. Central contribution
2. Reverse figure outline when Results are in scope
3. Paragraph storyboard
4. Unresolved evidence or decisions
